# Problem Statement

Customer: Health-conscious users, beginners in nutrition management, and individuals trying to improve fitness and weight outcomes.
Goal: Maintain a healthy lifestyle by following personalized nutrition plans and tracking daily intake consistently.
Problem: Users struggle to identify accurate calorie requirements, choose suitable meals, and monitor nutritional progress effectively.
Cause: Existing solutions are fragmented, generic, or do not provide integrated guidance with actionable tracking.
Impact: Inconsistent diet habits, poor nutritional balance, reduced motivation, and slower progress toward health goals.
