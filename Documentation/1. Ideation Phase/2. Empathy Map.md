# Empathy Map

Primary Persona: College students and working professionals (age 18–40) who want practical nutrition guidance within busy schedules.

## Think & Feel
- Wants to become healthier without following complex diet charts.
- Feels confused by conflicting nutrition advice online.
- Needs motivation and measurable progress to stay consistent.

## Hear
- Advice from doctors, fitness trainers, friends, and social media influencers.
- Frequent reminders that lifestyle diseases are increasing due to poor diet patterns.

## See
- Fast-food options are convenient and everywhere.
- Fitness apps that are either too generic or too complicated.

## Say & Do
- Says: “I need a practical daily plan that I can follow.”
- Checks calories occasionally but often forgets to track meals and water.
- Tries different diet plans but stops due to lack of personalization.

## Pain
- Difficulty understanding calorie needs and nutrient balance.
- Lack of continuity in tracking and accountability.
- No single platform that combines guidance, tracking, and progress analytics.

## Gain
- Clear and personalized meal suggestions aligned with goals.
- Easy monitoring of calories, macros, hydration, and weight trends.
- Confidence from measurable improvements shown on a dashboard.
